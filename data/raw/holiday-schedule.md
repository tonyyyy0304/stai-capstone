# Holiday Schedule and Special Days

## 2026 Company Holiday Calendar

Lumina Works observes all regular holidays and special non-working days proclaimed for the Philippines, plus company holidays listed below. Pay rules for work performed on holidays follow the Payroll and Compensation FAQ.

Proclamations issued by Malacañang during the year (additional special days) are announced by HR via email and observed accordingly.

## Company-Declared Holidays

December 24 and December 31 are paid company holidays for all employees. Employees required to work on a company-declared holiday due to client commitments receive a substitute leave day, to be used within ninety (90) days.

## Holiday Pay Eligibility

To be paid for a regular holiday, an employee must be present or on approved paid leave on the working day immediately before the holiday (the "holiday pay rule"). Employees on leave without pay the day before a regular holiday are not entitled to holiday pay for that day.

## Christmas Shutdown

The office closes from December 24 through January 1. Skeleton crews for critical client operations are arranged by Department Heads in November; volunteers are prioritized and receive holiday premium pay plus substitute leave per this policy.
