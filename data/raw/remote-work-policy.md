# Remote Work Policy

## Eligibility

Hybrid and remote arrangements are available to regular employees whose roles do not require physical presence (as classified by the Department Head) and who have completed probation. Probationary employees work on-site unless their offer letter states otherwise. Eligibility may be suspended for employees on a Performance Improvement Plan or with attendance-related disciplinary records in the last six (6) months.

## Hybrid Schedule

The default arrangement is hybrid: a minimum of two (2) office days per week, with Tuesday designated as the company-wide anchor day. Teams choose their second anchor day each quarter. Office attendance is logged via badge-in and reconciles against the HRIS; repeated failure to meet the minimum without approval is treated as an attendance violation under the Employee Handbook.

## Working Hours While Remote

Remote employees observe the same working hours and core collaboration hours (10:00 AM – 4:00 PM) as on-site staff, log time in the HRIS, and must be reachable on Slack during working hours. Overtime rules apply equally to remote work and require prior approval.

## Equipment and Allowances

The company issues a laptop to all employees; remote and hybrid employees also receive the PHP 1,000 monthly internet allowance described in the Benefits Guide. A one-time home office subsidy of PHP 8,000 (monitor, chair, or peripherals; receipts required) may be claimed once every three (3) years. Company equipment remains company property and must be returned upon separation or on request.

## Security Requirements

When working remotely you must: connect through the company VPN for all internal systems; use only company-managed devices for client and production data; enable full-disk encryption and screen lock (enforced by IT policy); and avoid public Wi-Fi for confidential work unless on VPN. Do not allow household members to use company devices. Report lost or stolen equipment to IT and HR within twenty-four (24) hours.

## Working Abroad

Short-term work from outside the Philippines is allowed up to thirty (30) calendar days per year, subject to written approval from your Department Head and HR at least two (2) weeks in advance, verification of data-privacy and client-contract constraints, and time zone overlap of at least four (4) hours with Manila core hours. Longer arrangements require Executive Committee approval and may have tax and immigration implications assessed case by case.

## Full-Remote Arrangements

Full-remote status (zero required office days) is exceptional and role-dependent. Requests are made in writing to the Department Head with a business justification, are reviewed by HR, and are re-evaluated every six (6) months. The company may recall full-remote employees to hybrid with thirty (30) days notice for business reasons.
