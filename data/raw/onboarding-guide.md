# Onboarding Guide

## Before Your First Day

Submit the following pre-employment requirements to HR at least three (3) working days before your start date, through the onboarding portal link in your offer email:

1. Signed employment contract
2. NBI clearance (issued within the last 6 months)
3. Photocopy of two (2) valid government IDs
4. SSS, PhilHealth, Pag-IBIG, and TIN numbers (or proof of application)
5. BIR Form 2316 from your previous employer (for mid-year hires)
6. Pre-employment medical exam results from an accredited clinic
7. PSA birth certificate
8. Bank account details for payroll (company payroll bank is BPI; enrollment assistance available)

Incomplete requirements may delay your start date or your first payroll credit.

## Your First Day

Report to the People Team desk at the 21st floor reception at 9:30 AM. Bring one valid government ID. Your first day includes:

- ID badge photo and building access card issuance
- Laptop and equipment issuance from IT (you will sign an accountability form)
- Account setup: company email, HRIS, Slack, and VPN
- Welcome lunch with your team
- Introduction to your onboarding buddy

Dress code on your first day is smart casual.

## Your First Week

| Day | Activity |
| --- | --- |
| Day 1 | HR orientation: policies, benefits enrollment, payroll setup |
| Day 2 | IT and security training (mandatory): passwords, VPN, phishing, data handling |
| Day 3 | Department orientation with your manager; role expectations and 30-60-90 plan |
| Day 4 | Product/domain walkthrough with your onboarding buddy |
| Day 5 | First-week check-in with HR; Q&A and feedback |

All mandatory trainings (IT security, Code of Conduct, anti-harassment) must be completed in the learning portal within your first fifteen (15) calendar days.

## Probationary Period

New hires undergo a probationary period of six (6) months. You will be evaluated against regularization standards communicated in writing on Day 3: role competencies, delivery of the 30-60-90 plan, and adherence to the Code of Conduct. Formal evaluations occur at the end of month three (3) and month five (5). Employees who meet the standards are regularized effective their six-month anniversary; regularization unlocks HMO coverage, leave usage, and the provident fund.

## Onboarding Buddy Program

Every new hire is paired with a tenured teammate for their first sixty (60) days. Your buddy is your first stop for "how do things work here" questions — tools, norms, who to ask about what. Buddies are not your evaluator and conversations with them are informal.

## Tools and Accounts

Standard accounts provisioned on Day 1: Google Workspace email, HRIS (leave filing, payslips), Slack, Jira, and VPN. Access to production systems and customer data requires your manager's request plus completion of the security training. If any account is not working by Day 2, file a ticket with IT support via the #it-help Slack channel.
